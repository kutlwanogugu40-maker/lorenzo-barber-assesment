Our Story

Kutlwano (Lorenzo) is a bustling barbershop located in the heart of Soweto, Protea North. What started as a small dream in 2019 has grown into a community staple.

After the challenges of the pandemic, we are Back & Better Since 2024. We believe that a haircut isn't just about hair—it's about confidence, culture, and community.

"We don't just cut hair; we craft identity."
